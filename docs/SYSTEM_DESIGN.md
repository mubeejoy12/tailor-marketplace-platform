# System Design

This platform connects customers with tailors.

Customers can order custom clothing designs.

---

# Marketplace Flow

Customer browses designs

↓

Customer selects design

↓

Customer submits measurements

↓

Customer places order

↓

Tailor receives order

↓

Tailor produces clothing

↓

Customer receives order

↓

Customer leaves review

---

# Trust System

Tailors gain reputation through:

ratings
completed orders
reviews

Higher rating increases visibility.
